// About

What Is This?

Glitch Radio is a side project of Aleph Void, LLC — music built out of malfunction, streaming while you work, reverse engineer, or just vibe.

📉

Sound From Failure

Glitch, IDM, and clicks and cuts — music made from the noise a system makes when it breaks. Corrupted samples, buffer errors, and bit rot, arranged on purpose.

📡

Always On

A continuous radio-style broadcast on Twitch. Drop in any time, leave it on in the background, and let the playlist do the work.
